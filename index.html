Frequently Asked Questions

What is Home Tutor Site ?

It is an online platform helping students and tutors find each other.

Can I post my learning needs for free ?

Yes, you can post your learning needs for free by registering as a free member.

Can I contact the tutors in our area whose skills match my needs?

Yes, you can contact our tutors you like by taking our premium membership

What are contact credits?

Contact credits are the keys to unlock the contact details. If you are a tutor looking for students, you can use contact credits to see the contact details of student/parent who posted the tuition needs

Do you have any money back guarantee?

Yes, we offer a money back guarantee if you did not find tutor/tuition in your plan period..

How do I pay my tuition fee?

You have to pay the fee to the tutor. 100% of the fee is for tutors and we do not take any commission from the tuition fee.

Do I need to pay any commission from my tuition fee?

No. We do not ask for any commission from tuition fee.
